Varying Snowflake

Introduction

The work interprets the uniqueness of snowflakes in nature as well as the beauty and variability of snowflakes in the artist’s imagination in the form of generative art and black and white aesthetics. The interaction with the mouse provides an interactive experience for the viewer and presents the condense, melt, and break of the snowflake.
